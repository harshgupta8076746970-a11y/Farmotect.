Business Description

 

Farmotech Ltd. was founded by Harsh Gupta, Kishan Dubey, and Suraj Maurya with the mission to revolutionize farming through robotics. As a company, we believe that agriculture, one of the most critical sectors in the world, is ripe for technological disruption. Our agricultural robot is designed to make farming operations more efficient by automating labour-intensive tasks.

The company is structured to ensure that each aspect of the business is covered by our team of dedicated experts. Kishan Dubey, our CEO and Operations head, leads the charge in promoting Farmotech’s vision and building partnerships. Harsh Gupta, as Financial Manager, oversees all financial operations, ensuring the company's economic sustainability. Suraj Maurya leads the Research and Development team, constantly innovating to improve our product and keep us ahead of the competition.

We are currently based in India and aim to expand operations globally as we gain traction in the domestic market. By focusing on automation, precision agriculture, and sustainability, we are positioning Farmotech to be a leader in the agricultural robotics industry.

 

 

 

 

EXecuTIVE SUMMARY

 

Farmotech Pvt. Ltd. is a forward-thinking company focused on transforming the agricultural industry through automation. Our flagship product is an advanced agricultural -KISAN BOT- designed to help farmers reduce labour costs, improve efficiency, and increase crop yields. As labour shortages and rising costs in agriculture persist, Farmotech offers a smart solution that tackles these challenges head-on. Our robot is capable of automating key processes like seeding, weeding, and irrigation, allowing farmers to focus on decision-making rather than manual tasks.

 

We are initially launching in selected Indian states, targeting progressive farmers and agricultural cooperatives. By demonstrating our robot’s capabilities at local farming expos and through digital marketing campaigns, we plan to build trust and awareness. Our financial projections show steady growth in revenue as we scale operations and reach more customers. With a focus on research and innovation, we aim to continuously improve our product to meet the evolving needs of modern agriculture. Our goal is to become a key player in the agricultural technology sector, providing cost-effective, efficient solutions that empower farmers to farm smarter, not harder.

 

 

 

 

 

Vision

 

Farmotech aspires to revolutionize the agricultural sector by enhancing its intelligence, efficiency, and sustainability. Our objective is to diminish reliance on manual labor while assisting farmers in increasing their yields and minimizing waste and resource consumption. We foresee a future where every farmer, irrespective of their operation's scale, can access advanced robotic technologies that simplify, expedite, and enhance the profitability of their work. In our inaugural year, we intend to secure a significant presence in the agricultural market by concentrating on specific regions within India. Our primary aim is to attain product-market alignment, ensuring that our robotic solutions effectively address the requirements of Indian farmers. Following this milestone, we will expand our operations to other states and ultimately to international markets. Farmotech is committed to aiding farmers in their transition to more intelligent farming practices that not only enhance productivity but also lessen environmental repercussions. Our overarching ambition is to emerge as a global frontrunner in agricultural robotics, spearheading innovations that will define the future of farming.

 

 

 

 

 

PRDUCT DECRIPITON

 

Farmotech's agricultural robot - KISANBOT represents a sophisticated and adaptable solution aimed at aiding farmers in the automation of essential agricultural tasks. This robot is capable of executing a diverse array of functions, such as spraying, tilling ploughing, grass cutting and  power source. Each of these capabilities is driven by cutting-edge sensors, enabling the robot to make informed decisions tailored to the specific requirements of the farm. Designed for user-friendliness, this product is accessible even to those farmers who may have limited experience with technology. It can be tailored to accommodate various farm sizes and types, making it appropriate for both small and large agricultural operations. Furthermore, the robot diminishes the dependency on human labor, which is increasingly becoming scarce and costly, particularly in rural regions. By automating labor-intensive processes, the robot empowers farmers to conserve time and concentrate on strategic planning, thereby enhancing productivity and profitability. Additionally, Farmotech's agricultural robot is engineered with sustainability as a core principle. It assists farmers in optimizing the use of resources such as water and fertilizers, thereby reducing waste and fostering environmentally responsible farming practices.

MARKET

 

The global agricultural robotics market is experiencing rapid expansion, propelled by the growing demand for efficiency in farming and the escalating costs associated with labor. In 2023, the market was valued at approximately USD 6.94 billion, with forecasts suggesting it could reach around USD 20 billion by 2029. This presents a substantial growth opportunity for companies operating in this sector, particularly those capable of providing innovative and cost-effective solutions like Farmotech. In India, the agricultural robotics market is still emerging but is on a steady upward trajectory. As of 2023, the market was valued at about USD 0.4 billion and is projected to grow to USD 1.2 billion by 2029. The Indian agricultural industry remains heavily reliant on manual labor, which is increasingly becoming a challenge.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


Target Audience

 

Farmotech primarily focuses on small and marginal farmers and agricultural cooperatives in India who are receptive to new technologies and are seeking methods to enhance efficiency, lower operational costs, and boost crop yields. Our agricultural robot is specifically designed to cater to small and medium-sized farmers, who frequently encounter labor shortages and escalating input costs. These farmers are particularly keen on solutions that enable them to optimize productivity without substantially increasing their operational expenditures. In addition to small and medium-sized farmers, we also aim to engage large-scale farming operations that necessitate automation for the effective management of vast agricultural fields. These larger enterprises possess the financial resources to invest in advanced technologies that facilitate the scaling of operations while either maintaining or reducing labor costs. Agricultural cooperatives and farming organizations represent another crucial segment of our target audience. These entities often play a significant role in shaping farming practices within their regions and can assist in promoting the adoption of Farmotech’s robot among their members. We expect our early adopters to emerge from states with high agricultural productivity and a growing interest in precision farming, such as Punjab, Maharashtra, and Haryana. As the advantages of our technology become increasingly apparent, we foresee a wider acceptance across various agricultural sectors.

 

 

Goal

 

Farmotech aspires to become a frontrunner in the agricultural automation sector by delivering cutting-edge robotic solutions that improve efficiency and productivity for farmers. In our inaugural year, we plan to distribute 200 units in strategic states such as Punjab, Haryana and Andhra Pradesh through focused marketing efforts, strategic partnerships, and practical demonstrations in the field. In the short term, our priority is to establish a solid product-market fit and cultivate a robust customer base. Looking ahead, we aim to secure 10% of the Indian market within five years and pursue international expansion, promoting the adoption of smart farming technologies that enhance sustainability and profitability in agriculture.
